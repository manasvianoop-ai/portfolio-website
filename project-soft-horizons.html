Capstone - Soft Horizons

2026 Conestoga College · Interior Decorating

Soft Horizons is a full residential Capstone project designed for Jada and Jordan, a newly married Millennial couple living in a 19th-floor waterfront condominium in Bronte Village, Oakville. Nestled beside Lake Ontario, the home is envisioned as a calm, light-filled retreat that supports clarity, comfort, and everyday ease. Inspired by the clients' love for travel, outdoor living, and hosting, the design embraces a warm contemporary style, combining clean lines with soft organic forms, layered lighting, and natural materials. Drawing inspiration from Mediterranean retreats, tropical destinations, and global influences, the home balances warmth, functionality, and refined elegance while creating a welcoming environment that fosters a strong sense of belonging.

Sustainability and craftsmanship were key values that guided the design process. Material selections throughout the entryway, open-plan living and dining area, kitchen, and bedrooms prioritize high-quality, durable, and environmentally conscious choices, including warm wood finishes, natural stone surfaces, and organic textiles. Layered textures and subtle geometric patterns add depth and visual interest while maintaining a calm and cohesive aesthetic. The layout is carefully planned to maximize natural daylight and take full advantage of the waterfront setting, strengthening the connection between the interior and its surroundings while reducing reliance on artificial lighting.

To address Jada's light sensitivity, the design incorporates layered lighting, matte finishes, diffused light sources, and motorized window treatments that provide greater control over natural daylight while minimizing glare. Pull-down storage features and motorized window treatments further reduce physical effort, improve accessibility, and support independent, long-term use.

Universal Design principles were an integral part of the project. With Jada's father, Dan (70), visiting frequently, the guest bedroom and bathroom were thoughtfully designed to support his current needs while remaining adaptable for future users. Barrier-free circulation, generous clear floor clearances, lever-style hardware, accessible fixture heights, pull-down storage solutions, and flexible features promote safety, comfort, and independence without compromising the overall aesthetic. Soft Horizons demonstrates that inclusive design and contemporary elegance can seamlessly coexist, resulting in a home that is timeless, functional, welcoming, and adaptable for people of all ages and abilities.
