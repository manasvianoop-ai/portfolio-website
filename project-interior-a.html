2025 Conestoga College · Interior Decorating

L-Shaped Kitchen — Model & Elevations

This transitional L-shaped, open-concept kitchen strikes a perfect balance between functionality and timeless style. With smart storage like the cloud-shaped corner pull-out shelf, basket-pullout shelf for onions and potatoes and spice shelf, elegant symmetry around the focal stove wall, and a quartz-countertop island that offers seating and added utility, the space is designed for both everyday use and entertaining.

Cozy Corner Café — Rendering

Cozy Corner Cafe may be small, but it's full of charm — tucked into a busy street corner, this little kiosk serves warmth in every cup, with its Oak wood fluted wooden panel, Calcatta marble-look porcelain countertop, handwritten menu, and the aroma of fresh coffee and croissants.
