True North Sparkling Water

2024 Conestoga College · Interior Decorating

TRUE NORTH is a sparkling water brand here to revolutionize your beverage game with its blend of real fruit flavours and satisfying bubbles! The brand positions itself as a guilt-free beverage option suitable for any occasion. The logo design draws inspiration from British Columbia's northern wilderness, incorporating visual elements from glacial valleys and soaring mountain peaks, to dense forests and sweeping wildlife parks.
