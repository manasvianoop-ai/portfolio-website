About

Manasvi Bhan is an Interior Decorator with a diploma from Conestoga College, based in Waterloo, Ontario. Her work spans residential interiors, brand identity, and styled photography — united by a belief that beauty and function are inseparable. She is drawn to spaces that feel considered, textured, and quietly alive.
