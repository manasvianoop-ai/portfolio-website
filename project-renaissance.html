Renaissance

2025 Conestoga College · Interior Decorating

The Renaissance — meaning "rebirth" — was a transformative period from the 14th to 17th century that redefined art, architecture, and human expression. Masters such as Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Raphael introduced unparalleled depth, realism, and beauty through a revival of classical proportion and form. Its legacy endures in contemporary design through symmetry, arched forms, rich ornamentation, and the timeless pursuit of balance — principles that continue to inspire interiors to this day.
