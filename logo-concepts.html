Option C  —  Circle Seal

Character — Heritage and authoritative — inspired by studio stamp seals. The circular treatment reads as a mark of craft and permanence. Best as a secondary brand element: photography watermark, portfolio stamp, packaging, or social profile picture rather than a primary nav logo.
