Brand Identity

Weaver Tales

2024 Conestoga College · Interior Decorating

A luxury brand identity for Weaver Tales — a handmade rug company rooted in heritage craftsmanship and timeless storytelling. The project delivers a complete brand kit: a logo suite with multiple variations, a typographic and colour system, an inspirational moodboard, and photography direction. Every element is crafted to evoke warmth, artisanal authenticity, and the rich tactile quality of hand-woven textiles.
